The Knight’s Tour is a mathematical puzzle that has endured over 1000 years.

It is a sequence of moves of a knight on a chessboard such that the knight visits every box only once.

This game is simple: Click boxes in L-shaped paths, like the knight in chess, and see if you can turn all the red boxes into white boxes without getting stuck!

To start the game, click on any red box. Then click on the next red box with a dot on it. You can't use the empty/white boxes to move around.

If you click on boxes you can't click it again. The game will finish when every red box has been turned into White boxes.

On the Screen, you will get three buttons.
• Restart : To restart the game
• Undo : Undo the last move
• Solve : Show the solution of the game.
